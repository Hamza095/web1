With proper care, the vacuum insulation will maintain its effectiveness for years. Many customers report excellent performance even after 5+ years of daily use.

We recommend hand washing your HydroFlask Pro to preserve the exterior finish. The bottle itself is dishwasher safe, but the cap should be hand washed.

The HydroFlask Pro comes in three sizes: 20oz (perfect for desks), 32oz (great for workouts), and 40oz (ideal for all-day adventures).
